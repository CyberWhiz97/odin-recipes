Chicken Alfredo

Home Page

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces fettuccine
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add sliced chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and no longer pink, about 5 minutes per side.
  4. Lower the heat and pour in heavy cream. Stir in Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Simmer for 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
