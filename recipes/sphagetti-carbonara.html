Spaghetti Carbonara

Home Page

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 ounces pancetta, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese together until well combined.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook pancetta until crispy, about 4 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Reduce heat to low and add the drained spaghetti to the skillet. Toss to coat in the pancetta fat.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water a little at a time until creamy.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper. Sprinkle with fresh parsley before serving.
